What is the BTS SIO SLAM?

The student of the BTS SIO option SLAM acquires the skills of three fields of activity, two of which are in common with the BTS SIO option SISR:


- Cybersecurity of IT services: the student is trained in cybersecurity and its integration into the company, taking into account the technical, organizational, legal dimensions ... He thus obtains skills in the protection of data and the digital identity of the company, securing the equipment and uses of users. SLAM option: he is trained in the cybersecurity of an application solution and its development.


- Support and provision of IT services: the student will be able to meet user expectations by ensuring the availability of existing IT services; he will also be able to take into account the IT needs in the company and support the digital transformation of IT services, while maintaining its employability. They thus acquire the skills to manage IT assets, respond to incidents, develop the company's presence on the Web, and organize their own professional development.


- SLAM option: Application design and development: the student will participate in the design, development, deployment and maintenance of the software components of an application solution. He thus acquires skills in the design and development of an application solution, in its maintenance, but also in databases and digital data management.
